Tomatino's Pizzeria

Traditional Italian Pizza Cooked in Clay Oven

In a highly afluent London Road in Thornton Heath, Tomatinos serves traditional Italian pizza, made with traditional Italian ingredients, cooked in a wood fired clay oven. We also have Italian cured meats and cheese available to buy from the Deli, where you can also drop in for coffee and biscotti.

The pizza served in Tomatinos, is exactly the same as the pizza you can eat on the streets of Italy, using the same traditional Italian ingredients and adopting the same authentic cooking method.
